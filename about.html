FC Barcelona (official name — Futbol Club Barcelona) is one of the most successful and recognizable football clubs in the world, founded in 1899. The club plays at the Camp Nou stadium, which is one of the largest stadiums in Europe.

Barcelona is famous for its unique style of play — "tiki-taka" — and for developing its own players in the La Masia academy, where legends such as Lionel Messi, Andres Iniesta, and Gerard Piqué were trained. The team has won the treble (La Liga, Copa Del Rey, and UEFA Champions League) twice, in the 2008-09 and 2014-15 seasons.

The club's biggest rival is Real Madrid, and their matches are called "El Clásico". These games are considered some of the most intense and popular in world football.

Currently, Barcelona is not just a football team but a sports club that includes basketball, handball, hockey, and other sports teams.

The Camp Nou stadium has a capacity of over 99,000 spectators, making it the largest stadium in Europe. This is where the club's most important matches take place, as well as significant events and concerts.

The FC Barselona have won 97 official titles. They are the most successful club in Spain, with 75 titles. Those domestic titles are 31 Copa del Ray , 26 La Liga , 13 Supercopa de España , 3 Copa Eva Duarte , and two Copa de la Liga . Internationally, the club is also one of the most successful. It has won 22 official titles, including two Latin Cup , three Inter-Cities Fairs Cup , five UEFA Champions League, four UEFA Super Cup, four UEFA Cup Winners' Cup , and three FIFA Club World Cup.
